Make What You Want

Tinypin is a minimal tool to create collections of links, or "boards". Simply add links into your collection, and they will be automatically embedded. Add articles, images, videos, audio, tweets, and more.

Make it yours

It's a simple framework, but your options are only limited by your creativity. There is no wrong way to use it! one Use tinypin to make buzzfeed style listicles, reading lists to share with your team, personal inspiration boards for your projects. You could even run a blog or a magazine.

Show it off

Share your boards, or don't. Make public boards, or hide your boards. If you create an account, you will be able to update and edit your boards. If you just need to throw something together quickly, thats cool too! You don't need an account to make a board and share it.
